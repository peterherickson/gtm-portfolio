## Cryptocurrency Dashboard
**Date:** January 2021

Calculates tax liability on cryptocurrency returns and tracks unrealized return on investment alongside daily pricing data. Automatically generates an interactive report from Coinbase transaction data.

**Technical Details:**
- Uses Google Sheets as a database
- Pricing and transaction data fed through APIs, updated daily
- Data blended in Google Data Studio using crypto asset symbol as shared key
- Google Sheets chosen for ease of setup and ability to merge transaction data from multiple wallets by adding new tabs

**Use Case:** Crypto trader with multiple assets and wallets who wants to track performance and realized gains over time.

## CRM Dash
**Date:** October 2020

Compares key performance metrics over time, between salespeople, lead sources, industries or virtually any other parameter in a CRM. Automatically generates an interactive report using opportunity or lead data from Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho or others.

**Problem:** Sales organizations struggle to dynamically report on key performance metrics within their CRMs. Native UI of platforms like Salesforce is inflexible, making it difficult to design dashboards that meet specific needs of executives. Data cleanup is cumbersome with hard-to-find errors hidden in a sea of records.

**Solution:** A good dashboard drives results, making it the go-to place for tracking progress and delivering the most pertinent information to executives. Gives the big picture while offering the option to quickly zoom into any range or record.

## Philly Artist Census
**Date:** November 2020

Visualizes comparisons of various demographic groups in Philadelphia's artist community. Using survey data collected from 449 respondents, this interactive report paints a picture of an average Philly artist's income, sales and exhibitions.

**Data Source:** Survey data gathered by Philly Stewards, an organization dedicated to improving Philadelphia's art ecosystem. Questions covered annual income, demographics, art sales and art exhibitions with an eye towards understanding what factors drive various measurements of success.

**Insights:** The data visualization demonstrates how certain demographic groups appear to experience more success than others. These insights can aid in understanding where gaps in access to education and opportunity exist.

## Expertscape
**Date:** November 2020

Compares medical research published by pharmaceutical institutions, with more than 29,000 unique topics. Provides a picture into which institutions lead in highly specific research topics, analyzing their output over time.

## Philly Gallery Survey
**Date:** January 2021

Analysis of Philadelphia's art gallery ecosystem in the wake of COVID-19, demonstrating a correlation with new sales and traffic to increased web presence. Aided in research towards article "The dis-located gallery: How 2020 moved Philly's contemporary art scene online."
