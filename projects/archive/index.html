Digital Art & Commerce

2019 - 2022

Peter Erickson Art — Shopify Store

eCommerce storefront and art portfolio showcasing 100+ artworks. Built on Shopify with Printful print-on-demand integration for automated fulfillment, shipping prints worldwide.

Fulfilled ~30 orders before the store was taken down. Printful handled printing and shipping automatically. Later migrated to Wix.

March 2021

First NFT Sale — Spacetime Crystal

Minted and sold digital artwork as NFT on Rarible. Listed for 0.15 ETH (~$250), sold within 2 hours to a collector interested in 'Astral Travel. Programming. Conspiracies.'

After fees: ~0.05 ETH net (paid $160 in gas/marketplace fees on $250 sale). Received in WETH, faced conversion costs and tax complexity. Lesson: gatekeepers still exist, just decentralized. Published writeup in Technical.ly.
